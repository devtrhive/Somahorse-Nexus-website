About Somahorse Nexus

Africa's greatest asset is its young, growing population. Our purpose is to convert this potential into progress. We build the infrastructure that connects Africa's technical talent with the complex challenges faced by its most important industries, creating a new engine for economic growth.

Somahorse Nexus is an integrated system with three core components: the Talent Foundry, the Industrial Solutions Hub, and the Capital & Impact Dashboard. We measure success by functional solutions and commercial readiness.

How the system works

Talent Foundry

Assessment -> real projects -> verification by deliverable. Graduates join a verified talent pool.

Industrial Solutions Hub

Catalog of AI blueprints matched to teams; we manage initiation to payment and delivery.

Capital & Impact Dashboard

Live KPIs: developer earnings, partner efficiency gains, and ecosystem health.
